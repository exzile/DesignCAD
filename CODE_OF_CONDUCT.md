# Code of Conduct

DesignCAD follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while staying open to correction.
- Help keep discussions focused and useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or targeted insults.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainers.
